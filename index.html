Overview

Pi-CADS – Motor is a next-generation, cloud-based, SaaS platform for 2D axisymmetric motor design and simulation. Built for both EV and non-EV applications, it serves engineers, manufacturers, researchers, and enthusiasts across India and global markets. With a rich suite of tools, real-time feedback, and intuitive design interfaces, Pi-CADS empowers users to create, simulate, and optimize electric motors with unmatched ease and accuracy.

Design motors for the vehicle by inputting key specifications like battery voltage, wheel radius, and gross weight. Define performance parameters, such as acceleration, max speed, and performance on gradients. Customize the vehicle’s drive cycle interactively by adjusting points on a Speed vs. Time graph for precise control.

  • Choose from topologies: IPMSM, SPMSM, or SynRM
  • Define geometry, materials, and simulation settings
  • Instantly access winding diagrams, flux plots, efficiency charts, and cost estimates
  • Vehicle + Motor Workflow
  • Interactive Drive Cycle Design
  • Real-Time Visual Outputs & Cost Estimation
